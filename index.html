Virat Kohli

Former captain of the Indian national cricket team

About the Legend

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. Kohli is known for his aggressive and dynamic style of play, exceptional batting skills, and strong leadership qualities.

Kohli made his international debut in August 2008, representing the Indian cricket team in a One Day International (ODI) match against Sri Lanka. He quickly established himself as a prolific run-scorer and rose through the ranks to become the captain of the Indian cricket team across all formats.

In Tests, Kohli has been a consistent performer, accumulating numerous centuries and leading the Indian team to significant victories both at home and abroad. He has also excelled in limited-overs cricket, particularly in ODIs, where he has achieved remarkable records. Kohli has scored multiple centuries and has played a crucial role in India's triumph in major tournaments like the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013 and the Asia Cup in 2018.

During his career, Kohli has received numerous accolades, including several ICC awards such as the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year and the ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year. He has also been recognized as one of the Wisden Cricketers of the Year.
